Key Components of Farmer Management

Data Management

Store, process, update, and manage farmer data including farms, crops, labor, and expenditures efficiently.

Crop & Resource Mgmt

Manage crop planning, planting schedules, and monitor soil health parameters like moisture and nutrients.

Market Access

Enable farmers to sell products online and purchase tools/seeds directly from sellers with better pricing insights.

User Management

Secure access for admins and farmers. Admins oversee operations while farmers manage their profiles.

Weather & Risk

Real-time weather forecasts and risk management tools to help farmers plan activities effectively.
